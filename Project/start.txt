On the Elder Plain two great cities stand aloof from the turmoultuous land. 
Boburg and Naridge exist apart from one another.
The road between fraught with trials that only the bravest of adventurers dare to undertake.
From Bandits on the Road, to the Falingual River, Goblins in the Calin Mountains, and the Cenote Desert.
This journey is not one to undertake lightly. 
For those willing and able however; the glory and riches that come from delivering your wares are enough to last many lifetimes.
